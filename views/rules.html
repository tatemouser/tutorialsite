How to Play!

  1. Score points by hitting the ball over the net and into the opponent's court.
  2. Only the serving team can score points.
  3. Games are typically played to 11 points, win by 2.
  1. The ball does not need to bounce before hitting, except after the serve when it must bounce once on each side.
  2. If the ball lands in the "kitchen" (non-volley zone), it must bounce before being hit.
  3. If the ball bounces twice on one side, the point is over.
  1. Must serve underhand.
  2. Keep one foot behind the baseline while serving.
  3. Must serve to the box diagonally opposite the server.
  4. Upon fault, the next player serves.
  5. A serve is considered a fault if it does not land in the diagonal box or hits the net.
  1. Games are typically played to 11 points, win by 2.
  2. Each player serves until a fault is made, then the serve passes to the next player.
  3. Call lines and faults fairly. Respect opponents and maintain good sportsmanship.
